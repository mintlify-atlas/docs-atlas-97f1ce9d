Better Notes (BN) is a plugin for [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org) that turns Zotero's built-in note editor into a full knowledge management system. It streamlines every stage of your research workflow — from reading and annotating papers to structuring knowledge and sharing it in the format you need — without ever leaving Zotero.

## Why Better Notes?

Researchers and students often juggle several tools: a reference manager, a note-taking app, and a writing environment. Better Notes collapses those into one. It works out of the box with sensible defaults, scales to highly customized workflows through JavaScript templates and the plugin API, and keeps everything inside Zotero where your library already lives.

Better Notes supports the following workflows:

- **Paper reading** — open unlimited note tabs and windows alongside your PDFs
- **Annotating** — convert PDF annotations to notes with one click
- **Note taking** — enhanced editor with a Magic Key command palette, outline view, and Markdown paste
- **Metadata analysis** — extract and compare fields across papers using templates
- **Knowledge exporting** — export to Markdown, DOCX, PDF, LaTeX, and FreeMind mind maps
- **AI writing assistance** — integrates with Zotero-GPT for in-editor AI support

## How it fits your workflow

Better Notes is designed to be combined like building blocks. You can use just the enhanced editor and note linking, or layer in templates, Markdown sync, and export to build a fully automated research pipeline. The plugin also exposes a [JavaScript API](/api/overview) that other plugin developers and power users can call to extend or automate note operations.

<Note>
  Better Notes targets Zotero 8 and 9. Make sure you are running a compatible version before installing.
</Note>
